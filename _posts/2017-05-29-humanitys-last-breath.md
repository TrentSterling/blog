**A game about submarines and nukes or something.**

Made this for **DIVEJAM 2017** - a game jam run by my friend Diver. Deep sea adventure vibes.

[Play it on itch.io](https://trent.itch.io/humanitys-last-breath-divejam)

## Controls

- **Mouse** - Aim submarine
- **Left Click** - Shoot
- **Right Click** - Thrust

## The Verdict

It's not very good. But hey, it's not that bad either.

One commenter said they "had the most laughs I didn't get in a long time playing this game" - so the humor landed at least.

Classic jam game - ambitious scope, questionable execution, probably some bugs. But it shipped!
