A Photon networked topdown twin-stick shooter planned for release on PC/Android.

## Technical Details

Preliminary testing shows that the game will run smoothly on the Fire Phone and the FireTV with shadows disabled.

## Goals

Hoping to get this game out quickly as all the artwork has been purchased from asset stores. Core networking and gameplay systems already functional.

## Platforms

- PC
- Android
- Amazon Fire Phone
- Amazon FireTV
