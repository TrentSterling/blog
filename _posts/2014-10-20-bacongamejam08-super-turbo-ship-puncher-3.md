A game created in 48 hours for the #BaconGameJam. The theme was “Millions Of Them”.

---

Dodge through 100’s of enemies while picking up 1000’s of pickup’s and scoring  millions!!!!

Collect colorful loot. Kills restore health. KEEP MOVING!

Try to stay alive by killing enemies and keeping your health above 0.

Sh’m’up powered by Unity3D.
---

#BaconGameJam is over, and the rating has begun. When the ratings are complete, I’ll be able to fix any bugs and balance gameplay a bit.

That laser is very overpowered.
---

 

WASD+Arrows. **360 controller** is recommended for twinstick gameplay.
